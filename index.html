About Megan

A familiar face, a warm home, a love of learning

I'm Megan — a mum of two and a home-based educator with three years of experience caring for tamariki in Invercargill. Before stepping into childcare, I spent years as a Children's Librarian at both Invercargill and Christchurch City Libraries, so books, stories and curious little minds have always been at the heart of what I do.

I operate under the Tiny Nation network, which means I receive ongoing professional support and work within a framework that keeps your child's wellbeing and learning at the centre of everything.

My Philosophy

"Learning through play creates quicker brain connections than learning in isolation — so we follow the child's lead, always."

I'm a strong believer in child-led learning. I follow and expand on the interests shown by each tamariki in my care, trusting that when a child is genuinely engaged, real learning happens naturally.

🧭

Child-Led Learning

Every child has unique interests and passions. I follow their curiosity and build rich learning experiences around what matters to them.

🎨

Learning Through Play

Play is the most powerful tool a child has. It builds brain connections, social skills, creativity and resilience all at once.

🌱

Community & Socialisation

Through Playcentre, community outings and shared experiences, tamariki build the confidence to engage with the wider world.
